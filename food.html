Food

The countries from the last page have a lot of good food. Spain has Tortilla de Patatas which is Spanish Omelette. Mexico has Pozole which is a soup. Colombia has Arepa which is flat bread made with cornmeal. Lastly, Costa Rica has gallo pinto which is rice and beans.
